## Introduction

Welcome to Prescripto, a comprehensive medical appointment reservation system. This manual guides you through all features and functionality available to patients and healthcare professionals using the platform.

Prescripto simplifies the process of finding qualified doctors and scheduling medical appointments. Whether you're looking for a specialist or need a routine checkup, this platform makes it easy to find the right healthcare professional and book your appointment in minutes.

### What You Can Do

- Browse and search for qualified doctors
- Filter by medical specialty, fees, and availability
- View detailed doctor profiles and credentials
- Book appointments at convenient times
- Manage your appointment history
- Communicate with administrators through the chat interface
- Receive real-time updates about your appointments

## Getting Started

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

## Registration and Authentication

### Creating Your Account

**Step 1: Access Registration**
1. On the login screen, look for "Don't have an account?" or "Sign Up" link
2. Click to proceed to registration

**Step 2: Enter Basic Information**
- **Full Name**: Your complete name
- **Email Address**: Active email address (used for login and communications)
- **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters recommended)
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

**Step 3: Complete Registration**
1. Review the terms and conditions
2. Click "Register" or "Create Account"
3. Your account will be created immediately
4. You'll be redirected to the login screen

**Step 4: Login**
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Login"
4. You're now accessing your patient dashboard

### OAuth Authentication

Prescripto also supports quick registration using social media accounts:

**Google Login:**
1. Click "Login with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permissions (if requested)
4. Your account is created and you're logged in

**Facebook Login:**
1. Click "Login with Facebook"
2. Select your Facebook account
3. Grant permissions
4. Your account is created and you're logged in

**Benefits of OAuth:**
- Quick registration without creating a new password
- Secure authentication
- Automatic account creation

### Resetting Your Password

**If You Forgot Your Password:**
1. On the login screen, click "Forgot password?"
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link in the email
6. Enter your new password
7. Confirm the new password
8. Click "Reset Password"
9. Return to login and use your new password

**Important:** Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security.

### Updating Your Profile

**Access Profile Settings:**
1. Click on your user menu (top right)
2. Select "My Profile" or "Profile Settings"

**Update Personal Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Gender
- Date of birth
- Address (street/suburb)

**Profile Photo:**
1. Click "Upload Photo" or "Change Photo"
2. Select image from your device
3. The image is uploaded and becomes your profile picture

**Save Changes:**
After making updates, click "Save Changes" or "Update Profile"

## Booking an Appointment

### Starting the Booking Process

1. Find your preferred doctor (using search and filters)
2. Click "View Profile"
3. On the doctor's profile, click "Book Appointment"
4. You'll proceed to the appointment booking form

![Appointment Flow Diagram](./images/appointment-flow.svg)

### Step 1: Select Appointment Date

1. A calendar will appear showing available dates
2. Dates with availability are highlighted/clickable
3. Click your preferred date
4. The selected date is highlighted

**Date Selection Tips:**
- Only future dates can be selected
- Grayed-out dates indicate no availability
- You can navigate through months using arrows

### Step 2: Select Appointment Time

1. After selecting a date, available time slots appear
2. Time slots are typically in 30-minute increments
3. Click your preferred time slot
4. The selected time is highlighted

**Time Slot Information:**
- Green/highlighted slots are available
- Times are shown in your local timezone
- Popular slots may fill quickly

### Step 3: Enter Your Information

Ensure your personal details are correct:

**Patient Information (Pre-filled):**
- Name: Your full name
- Email: Your registered email
- Phone: Your contact number

**Verify Information:**
1. Confirm all details are correct
2. Update if any information has changed
3. Ensure phone number is current for notifications

### Step 4: Appointment Summary

Review all details before confirming:

**Appointment Details:**
- Doctor name and specialty
- Date and time
- Consultation fees
- Appointment duration (typically 30 minutes)
- Total cost

### Step 5: Payment and Confirmation

**Payment Information:**
1. Payment method is displayed (Stripe)
2. Click "Proceed to Payment" or "Pay Now"
3. You're redirected to the secure payment portal

**Credit Card Payment:**
1. Enter credit card number
2. Enter expiration date
3. Enter CVV (3-digit security code)
4. Enter cardholder name and billing address
5. Click "Pay" or "Complete Payment"

**After Payment:**
1. Payment is processed securely
2. Confirmation screen displays
3. Confirmation email is sent to your registered email
4. Your appointment is now booked

### Booking Confirmation

After successful booking:

**Confirmation Details:**
- Appointment reference number
- Doctor information
- Date and time
- Location/address
- Confirmation code
- Payment receipt

**Confirmation Email:**
You receive an email containing:
- All appointment details
- Doctor's contact information
- Pre-appointment instructions (if any)
- Directions to the office
- How to reschedule or cancel

## Managing Appointments

### Accessing Your Appointments

1. Click "My Appointments" in the main navigation
2. You see a list of all your appointments
3. Appointments are organized by status (upcoming, completed, cancelled)

![Appointment Sequence Diagram](./images/appointment-sequence.svg)

### Appointment Status

**Upcoming:**
- Scheduled appointments in the future
- Shows date, time, and doctor information

**Completed:**
- Past appointments that have been completed
- Includes completion date and time

**Cancelled:**
- Appointments you have cancelled
- Shows cancellation date and reason

### Appointment Details

Click on any appointment to view:
- Doctor information and contact details
- Appointment date and time
- Location/office address
- Payment amount and status
- Appointment reference number
- Any appointment notes

### Rescheduling an Appointment

**To Reschedule:**
1. Open your appointments list
2. Find the appointment to reschedule
3. Click "Reschedule" button
4. Follow the same booking process to select new date/time
5. Confirm the new appointment
6. Original appointment is automatically cancelled

**Important:** Rescheduling may require additional payment if fees differ.

### Cancelling an Appointment

**To Cancel:**
1. Open your appointments list
2. Find the appointment to cancel
3. Click "Cancel Appointment"
4. A confirmation dialog appears
5. Enter cancellation reason (optional)
6. Click "Confirm Cancellation"

**After Cancellation:**
- Appointment status changes to "Cancelled"
- You receive cancellation confirmation email
- Refund policy applies (check your system's refund terms)

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Appointments can typically be cancelled up to 24 hours before
- Late cancellations may be subject to fees
- Emergency cancellations may have restrictions

### Appointment Reminders

**Email Reminders:**
- Sent 24 hours before appointment
- Sent 2 hours before appointment

**In-App Notifications:**
- Appear when you log in
- Show upcoming appointments
- Provide quick access to appointment details

Prescripto offers two chat modes:

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Issue: Forgot Password

**Problem:** Can't remember your login password

**Solution:**
1. Click "Forgot password?" on login screen
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
4. Click the reset link
5. Create a new password
6. Log in with new password

---

#### Issue: Payment Failed

**Problem:** Credit card payment was rejected

**Possible Causes:**
- Incorrect card number
- Expired card
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect CVV
- Card blocked by bank

**Solution:**
1. Return to booking page
2. Verify all card details are correct
3. Try different payment method if available
4. Contact your bank to ensure card is active
5. Use support chat to contact assistance

---

#### Issue: Can't Find Doctor

**Problem:** Cannot locate a specific doctor

**Solution:**
1. Use the search bar to find by name
2. Try searching by specialty
3. Clear all filters to see full doctor list
4. Check if doctor is marked as unavailable
5. Contact support if you believe the doctor should be listed

---

#### Issue: Appointment Not Confirmed

**Problem:** After payment, no confirmation appears

**Solution:**
1. Wait 5-10 minutes (system may be processing)
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your email for confirmation
4. Log in and check "My Appointments"
5. Contact support with your email and payment info

---

#### Issue: Can't Update Profile

**Problem:** Changes to personal information won't save

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache
4. Logout and login again
5. Try updating one field at a time
6. Contact support if issue persists

---

#### Issue: Chat Not Working

**Problem:** Cannot access chat support

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Ensure chat widget is visible (bottom-right)
4. Try a different browser
5. Clear browser cache
6. Contact support directly via email if unavailable

## FAQ

### Account and Authentication

**Q: Is it safe to create an account with Google/Facebook?**
A: Yes, OAuth authentication is secure and industry-standard. We do not store your social media passwords.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Each person should have one account. Multiple accounts with the same information may be merged.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support through the chat interface. Your data will be deleted according to our privacy policy.

**Q: Can I change my registered email?**
A: Yes, update it in your profile settings. You'll need to verify the new email before changes take effect.

---

### Doctors and Appointments

**Q: How far in advance can I book an appointment?**
A: Typically, appointments can be booked up to 30 days in advance, depending on doctor availability.

**Q: Can I book an appointment for someone else (family member)?**
A: Currently, each person must create their own account to book appointments.

**Q: What if I need an urgent appointment?**
A: Look for doctors marked as "Available Now" or use support chat to request urgent appointments.

**Q: Are appointment times guaranteed?**
A: Appointments are confirmed based on availability. Doctors may occasionally need to reschedule for emergencies.

---

### Payments and Fees

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) via Stripe.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed through Stripe with PCI-DSS compliance.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies depend on your system's settings. Check the terms or contact support.

**Q: Why was I charged if I cancelled?**
A: If you cancelled within the cancellation window, no charge applies. If outside, cancellation fees apply.

---

### Technical Issues

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Use the latest version for best results.

**Q: Can I use Prescripto on my phone?**
A: Yes, the platform is responsive and works on mobile devices.

**Q: What should I do if the page won't load?**
A: Clear your browser cache, try a different browser, or contact support.

**Q: Can I save appointments as a reminder?**
A: Email reminders are sent automatically 24 hours and 2 hours before appointments.

---

### Support and Chat

**Q: How long does it take to get support?**
A: Assistant mode is instant. Support team typically responds within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I request a specific doctor if unavailable?**
A: Use support chat to inquire about alternative dates or doctor recommendations.

**Q: How do I provide feedback about the system?**
A: Use the support chat to submit feedback. We value your input!

**Q: What are your support hours?**
A: Assistant mode is available 24/7. Support team operates during business hours.

---

### Data and Privacy

**Q: How is my personal data used?**
A: Your data is used only for appointment management and communication. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I export my appointment history?**
A: Contact support to request your data in standard format.

**Q: Is my medical information stored?**
A: Prescripto stores only contact and appointment information, not medical records.
